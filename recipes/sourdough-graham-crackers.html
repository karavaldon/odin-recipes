Sourdough Graham Crackes

Ingredients

  • 100g Sourdough Starter
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 55g Whole Wheat Flour
  • 10g Salt
  • 1 Cup Light Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon Honey

Recipe

  1. Mix all ingerdients in a bowl until combined.
  2. Let dough rest for 1 hour in the fridge.
  3. Roll dough into thin, 1/4 sheet and cut into rectangles.
  4. Place rectangles on to baking sheet and make holes with a fork.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes at 375.
