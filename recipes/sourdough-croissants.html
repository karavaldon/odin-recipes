Sourdough Croissants

Ingredients

  • 100g Sourdough Starter
  • 400g Flour
  • 200g Butter
  • 10g Salt
  • 20g Sugar
  • 50g Milk
  • 1 egg

Recipe

  1. Combine all ingredients except butter in a large bowl and rest overnight in the fridge.
  2. Roll into a rectangle and fold into thirds, then rest in the fridge for 1 hour.
  3. Roll cold butter between 2 sheets of plastic wrap into a sheet, and refridgerate for 30 minutes.
  4. Sandwich the dough and butter together, and fold into thirds, then refriderate for 1 hour.
  5. Repeat refrideration and folds 4 times.
  6. After the 4th fold, cut dough into triangular strips and roll.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes at 375.
