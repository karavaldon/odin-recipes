Sourdough Bread

Ingredients

  • 100g Sourdough Starter
  • 400g Bread Flour
  • 55g Whole Wheat Flour
  • 10g Salt
  • 345g Water

Recipe

  1. Mix soursough starter, flour, salt and water in a bowl until combined.
  2. Let dough rest for 1 hour, then "pull" one side up and fold it over on top of the dough. Repeat until you make a ball.
  3. Repeat the 1 hour rest, and pull/fold 3 times.
  4. After the 4th pull and fold place the dough on a floured surface and shape into a tight ball.
  5. Place the dough shooth side down into your basket and rest in the fridge for 8-24 hours.
  6. When you're ready to bake, cut the dough down the midle with a razor blade and place into a dutch oven.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes at 475, remove the lid and bake for 25 minutes at 450.
